Okay, so here is the base scenario.

You and your friend are members of a criminal gang. You both are arrested and imprisoned. Both of you are in solitary confinement which means you can't talk to your friend. The prosecutors lack sufficient evidence to convict you both on the principal charge, but they hope to get both of you sentenced to a year in prison on a lesser charge. The prosecutors offer you a bargain. Both of you are given two options:

Either betray the other by testifying that the other committed the crime.

Or cooperate with the other by remaining silent.

Note that you won't know what your friend will be choosing since you both can't talk.
