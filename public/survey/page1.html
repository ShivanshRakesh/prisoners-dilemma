Hey! Welcome to the survey! So you will be given 4 situations based on a common scenario. For each situation, there will be 2 questions, which you need to answer.

That's all! Simple, right?


So let's get started!

Okay, so here is the base scenario.

You are member of a criminal gang. You and an other person from the gang are arrested as suspects for a crime. Both of you are in solitary confinement which means you can't talk to each other. The prosecutors lack sufficient evidence to convict on the principal charge, but they hope to get both of you sentenced to a year in prison on a lesser charge. The prosecutors offer you two a bargain, which will be described later. Both of you have 2 options for each such offer:

Either betray the other by testifying that the other committed the crime.

Or cooperate with the other by remaining silent.

Note that you won't know what the other will be choosing since you both can't talk.




Situation ONE.

You are given the following offer:

If you both cooperate, you both go to prison for 1 year (on the lesser charge).

If you betray and the other cooperates, the other person will be going to prison for 3 years, and you will be set free (and vice-versa).

If you both betray each other, you both go to prison for 2 years.




Situation TWO.

You are given the following offer:

If you both cooperate, you both go to prison for 1 month (on the lesser charge).

If you betray and the other cooperates, the other person will be going to prison for 5 months, and you will be set free (and vice-versa).

If you both betray each other, you both go to prison for 2 years.




Situation THREE.

You are given the following offer:

If you both cooperate, you both would be set free.

If you betray and the other cooperates, the other person will be going to prison for 3 years, and you will be going to prison for 1 year (and vice-versa).

If you both betray each other, you both go to prison for 3 years.




Situation FOUR.

You are given the following offer:

If you both cooperate, you both would be set free.

If one of you betrays and the other cooperates, the police decides on what to do.

If you both betray each other, you both go to prison for 2 years.
